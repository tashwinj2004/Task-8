- Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):
	Displayed at the top of the dashboard to highlight total revenue and net profit, offering a quick snapshot of overall business performance.
- Monthly Revenue Trend – Line Chart:
	A dynamic line chart illustrates the month-over-month revenue trend, helping identify seasonal patterns, growth trajectories, and anomalies over time.
- Regional Revenue Distribution – Bar Graph:
	A comparative bar graph breaks down revenue by region, enabling clear insights into geographic performance and regional contribution to total sales.
- Order Volume by Product Category – Donut Chart:
	A visually engaging donut chart showcases the distribution of order counts across product categories, making it easy to spot top-performing segments.
